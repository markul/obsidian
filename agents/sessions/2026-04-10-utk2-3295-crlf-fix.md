## Actions

2. Added `.gitattributes` at repo root:
   - `* text=auto`, `*.sh text eol=lf`, `*.bat text eol=crlf`
3. Added `sed -i 's/\r$//' ./db-init/init-db.sh` to `Dockerfile` `RUN` step before `chmod +x`
